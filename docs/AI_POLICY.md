# AI Usage Policy

AI assistance is allowed in this repository.

It is also auditable.

## You may use AI for

- brainstorming approaches,
- generating boilerplate,
- drafting tests,
- refactor suggestions,
- documentation help,
- debugging assistance.

## You may not do

- submit code you cannot explain,
- hide meaningful AI usage,
- claim AI-produced work as fully manual,
- bypass repository rules by pasting in proprietary or credentialed material.

## Minimum disclosure standard

Your PR must clearly describe:
1. which AI tool(s) you used,
2. which files or sections were AI-assisted,
3. what the AI got wrong or incomplete,
4. what you changed manually,
5. and how you verified the final behavior.

## Interview rule

Reviewers may open any file you changed and ask:
- "What does this function do?"
- "Why this design?"
- "What part came from AI?"
- "What did you reject?"

If you cannot answer that, the submission will be treated as unverified.
