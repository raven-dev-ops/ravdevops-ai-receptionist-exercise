# Interview Part B: Explanation Review

After the coding submission, candidates should expect a live explanation review.

Typical prompts:
- Walk me through the request lifecycle.
- Show me where retrieval happens.
- How do you avoid unsupported assumptions?
- How does scheduling work?
- What did AI generate?
- What did you change and why?
- What would you do next in production?

This is why small, explainable changes beat large, opaque rewrites.
