# FAQ

## Can candidates use AI?
Yes. They must disclose and explain that usage.

## Is this an open source repo?
No. It is a public interview exercise.

## Can outside contributors submit PRs?
They can technically open them, but solution PRs are only reviewed from invited candidate collaborators.

## Why is the starter implementation conservative?
Because the exercise is designed around incremental improvements through issues rather than handing candidates a blank repo.
